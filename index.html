Super Deepfake Detector

Bringing Deepfake Detection to the browser.

Drop Video or Click to Upload

How many times have you seen a video of some celebrity saying some outlandish thing, thinking to yourself "this is has to be fake, right?" SuperDeepfakeDetector.com aims to solve that, by allowing users to drag and drop videos to detect deepfake videos, all in the browser. This allows for the use of deepfake detection, without the need for expensive hardware or expertise in the field. We are training and porting over models to run on the clientside, focusing on lightweight models which will run efficiently on the web.
